Hi! This is Biting Huang.

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Software, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. [Fei He](https://feihe.github.io). I am a member of the [THUFV](https://thufv.github.io) group and received my Bachelor's degree from the same department at Tsinghua University. My research interests include formal verification, program analysis, and programming languages, with a particular focus on termination analysis of programs.
